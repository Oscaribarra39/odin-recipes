Hot Cakes

This recipe is a classic, not recommended if you are diabetic, but tasty for everybody, it consist in a spongy and butterfull bread that you can cover whit any toping you like. Most common topics are condensed milk (also my favourite), strawberry jam, etc. And you can also add some fruit to make it even tastier and nutritious!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 of a cup of milk
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 a spoon of butter
  • 3 drops of vanilla

Steps

  1. First pour the flour in a medium size bowl
  2. Whit help of a cup, pour 3/4 of the cup of milk whitin the bowl
  3. Add the egg and stir the three Ingredients until there are no visible lumps
  4. In a pan, melt the butter, once is melted, add it to the mixture
  5. Use the same pan whit the remaining butter and pour the mixture into a circular shape
  6. Turn it around once you see bubbles in the mixture
  7. Repeat the same process to make a pile of hotcakes, add the topping and ther you go
